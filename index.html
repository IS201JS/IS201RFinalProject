God First

I truly believe God has given us so much in our lives. I think it is only fair that we do our best to follow his teachings given to the ancient and modern prophets. I am proud to say I am a firm believer in my God. He lives. He will come again.

Family Second

I think sometimes we get mixed up with what is actually important in life. As I have matured throughout my life i have come to relize there is no love compared to the love my mother has for me. There is no sacrifice that is compared to the sacrifice of my father. They gave the world to me and taught me how to workhard and love others. Family comes right after God.

Country Third

We live in the most blessed land in the world. The United States of America has been divinely protected by the hand of God for our generation. This is the land of the brave and of freedom. There is and never will exist another country like this. It is our duty to protect our freedom and rights for the next generation.
